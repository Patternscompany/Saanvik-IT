Kubernetes Training

At Saanvik IT Coaching Center, Kubernetes training focuses on equipping individuals and teams with the knowledge and skills necessary to effectively deploy, manage, and scale containerized applications using Kubernetes. Here's an overview of what Kubernetes training typically covers Understanding the fundamental concepts of Kubernetes, including pods, deployments, services, and namespaces. Exploring the architecture of Kubernetes clusters, including the control plane components (such as the API server, scheduler, and controller manager) and worker nodes. Learning how to set up and configure Kubernetes clusters on various platforms, including on-premises, cloud providers (like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud), and managed Kubernetes services (like AKS, EKS, and GKE). Understanding how to deploy, update, and scale containerized applications using Kubernetes manifests, including deployment YAML files and Helm charts.

Kubernetes training can be delivered through various formats, including instructor-led courses, hands-on workshops, online tutorials, and self-paced learning resources. It aims to empower individuals and teams with the knowledge and skills needed to leverage Kubernetes effectively for deploying and managing containerized applications in production environments.

"Kubernetes allows you to run and manage containerized applications at scale, with automated deployment, scaling, and management of application containers across clusters of hosts."
